PRO TIP FROM THE STUDIO

Tattoo & Piercing Aftercare Guide

We care about your ink long after you leave our studio. Follow these steps for perfect healing every time.

DOWNLOAD FULL AFTERCARE PDF

First 48 Hours

  • Keep the area clean & dry
  • Apply thin layer of aftercare balm
  • No direct sun or swimming

Days 3–14

  • Moisturize 3–4 times daily
  • Avoid tight clothing on tattoo
  • Do not scratch or peel

Mehandi Aftercare

  • Keep paste on for 6–8 hours
  • Apply oil after removal for darker stain
  • Avoid water for 24 hours

Questions? Our team is always available on WhatsApp for personalized healing advice.
